Our Origin Story

It started, as all great things do, with someone saying "we should really start working out more." Nobody remembers who said it. Nobody takes credit for it either. What we do know is that one fateful morning, a group of friends showed up at the same spot, realized none of them trained for this, and decided that sticking together was the only way any of them would finish.

The Mighti Duckz were born — a ragtag band of joggers, power walkers, and one guy who insists he's "active resting." We don't discriminate on pace. We discriminate on attitude. Show up. Suffer together. Celebrate everything.

The Duckz Code

These are our laws. We take them seriously. Mostly.

  1. 01
    No Duck Left Behind. The group finishes together. Always. The fast ones come back. The slow ones keep moving. Nobody gets left on the course.
  2. 02
    Showing Up is the Win. You ran today. That's the whole thing. The pace, the distance, the conditions — all secondary. You showed up. You're a Duck.
  3. 03
    Celebrate Everything. New personal best? Celebrate. First time finishing a route? Celebrate. Remembered to bring water? That's worth a group text at minimum.
  4. 04
    Complaining is Allowed, Quitting is Not. You may absolutely tell us how hard this is. We will agree. We will also keep running.
  5. 05
    The Post-Run Meal is Sacred. We earned it. We will enjoy it. No calorie math. No exceptions.

Think You're a Duck?

The Mighti Duckz don't recruit based on speed, experience, or talent. We recruit based on willingness to show up and a decent sense of humor about how hard this all is.

If that sounds like you — reach out. Lace up. We'll save you a spot at the back of the pack (it's the best spot, honestly).
